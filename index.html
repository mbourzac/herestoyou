Sheryl Bourzac is a Health Coach with over 30 years experience in the medical field. After receiving her BS in Nursing in 1994 she spent over a decade working as a Public Health Nurse for Kent County where she honed her skills delivering one on one medical advice to the city’s at risk populations. In 2005 she completed her Masters of Science in Nursing to further her skills and better understand our relationship with nutrition. As your Health Coach, she will bring that level of expertise to you on a personal level. When you’re ready, you can schedule your first phone consultation here.
